About Us

Nestled high in the breathtakingly beautiful snow-covered peaks, Alpine Peaks Resort is your ultimate winter wonderland getaway. We are not just a resort; we're an immersive experience, offering you a thrilling blend of adventure and relaxation. Whether you're an adrenaline junkie, a nature enthusiast, or simply seeking tranquility amidst the snow-capped mountains, Alpine Peaks has something for everyone.


If you're a snowsports enthusiast, you're in for a treat. Our resort boasts some of the best skiing and snowboarding slopes in the region. With meticulously groomed trails for all skill levels, from beginners to experts, you can carve down the mountainside with confidence. Lessons and equipment rentals are available, ensuring that everyone can join in the snowy fun.
